The Voice of the Mountains

Henry John Deutschendorf Jr., known to the world as John Denver, brought the spirit of the American West into our hearts and homes. With his gentle voice and honest lyrics, he painted pictures of Colorado skies, mountain streams, and the simple beauty of country life.

"I want to live, I want to give, I've been a miner for a heart of gold."
